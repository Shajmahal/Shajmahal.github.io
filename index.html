$ Who Am I?
I’m a Computer Engineering student who enjoys building things and understanding how they work at a deeper level. I’m most interested in projects where I can take an idea from concept to something real, whether that’s a circuit board, embedded firmware, or a small application. I care about the details and don’t like leaving problems half solved. I’m still growing as an engineer, but I approach every project with curiosity, discipline, and consistency.



$ what I build
I build practical systems — hardware, embedded code, and software applications — that are reliable, clearly structured, and tested properly. I focus on turning ideas into working results instead of just prototypes that look good on the surface.



$ my standard
I plan before building, test while developing, and document what I learn along the way. I try to understand the fundamentals first so when something breaks, I can fix it confidently instead of guessing. I care more about doing something correctly than rushing to call it finished.
