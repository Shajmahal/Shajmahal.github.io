$ who am i
I’m a Computer Engineering student who enjoys building things and understanding how they work at a deeper level. I’m most interested in projects where I can take an idea from concept to something real, whether that’s a circuit board, embedded firmware, or a small application. I care about the details, and I don’t like leaving problems half solved. I’m still growing as an engineer, but I approach every project with curiosity, discipline, and consistency.

$ what i focus on
I focus on building systems that are reliable and thoughtfully designed. That means planning before I build, testing as I go, and documenting what I learn along the way. I try to understand the fundamentals first so when something breaks, I can solve it confidently instead of guessing.

$ how i work
I break complex problems into smaller parts and solve them step by step. I test early, refine often, and make sure I can clearly explain what I built and why I built it that way. I care more about doing something correctly and understanding it fully than rushing to call it finished.
